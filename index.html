About

Alienation DPS Calculator is a small app designed to let you experiment with and optimize your weapon upgrades in the PS4 game Alienation.

Windows, Mac and Linux versions are provided, and it should also be buildable for any other platform that Lazarus supports.

Current version

The current version is 1.0.
